Our Dental Services

Comprehensive, compassionate care for every smile. Explore our full range of general, cosmetic, and specialty treatments below.

Our Services

1. Dental Exams & Cleanings

Routine check-ups are the cornerstone of good oral health. Our thorough exams include oral cancer screening, gum disease assessment, and digital X-rays. Professional cleanings remove plaque and tartar that regular brushing can't, preventing cavities and gum disease.

Price: 15 OMR

2. Dental Fillings

We use tooth-colored, composite fillings to restore teeth damaged by decay back to their normal shape and function. This quick treatment stops decay in its tracks and blends seamlessly with your natural tooth color.

Price: 20 OMR

3. Root Canal Therapy

When the inner pulp of a tooth becomes infected or inflamed, a root canal can save it. This procedure relieves pain, eliminates infection, and allows you to keep your natural tooth instead of having it extracted.

Price: 100 OMR

4. Tooth Extractions

We perform safe and gentle tooth removals when a tooth is too damaged to be saved, or to relieve crowding. We focus on your comfort throughout the process.

Price: 50 OMR

5. Night Guards & Mouthguards

Custom-fitted night guards protect your teeth from grinding and clenching. We also create protective mouthguards for athletes.

Price: 25 OMR

6. Teeth Whitening

Professional in-office or take-home whitening safely lifts years of stains from coffee, tea, wine, and tobacco, revealing a brighter smile.

Price: 60 OMR

7. Dental Veneers

Veneers are thin shells of porcelain bonded to the front of teeth. They correct discolored, chipped, misaligned, or gapped teeth for a flawless appearance.

Price: 150 OMR

8. Dental Bonding

Using tooth-colored composite resin, we repair chipped, cracked, or discolored teeth in a single visit, improving aesthetics cost-effectively.

Price: 70 OMR

9. Dental Crowns

A crown is a custom-made cap that fully covers a damaged tooth, restoring its shape, size, strength, and appearance.

Price: 120 OMR

10. Dental Bridges

A bridge replaces missing teeth by anchoring artificial teeth to healthy adjacent teeth, restoring chewing, speech, and facial shape.

Price: 200 OMR

11. Dental Implants

A dental implant is a permanent solution for tooth loss. A titanium post is placed in the jawbone, topped with a crown that looks and functions like a natural tooth.

Price: 400 OMR

12. Dentures

We offer full and partial dentures to replace multiple missing teeth. Custom-crafted for comfort and natural appearance.

Price: 250 OMR

13. Orthodontics (Braces & InvisalignÂ®)

We straighten teeth using braces or Invisalign. Proper alignment leads to an attractive smile, easier cleaning, and long-term oral health.

Price: 1200 OMR

14. Pediatric Dentistry

Gentle care for children, including first dental visits, preventive cleanings, fluoride treatments, and sealants. Positive experiences for lifelong healthy habits.

Price: 30 OMR

15. Periodontal (Gum) Therapy

We diagnose and treat gum disease, from early gingivitis to advanced periodontitis. Includes deep cleanings and infection removal for gum health.

Price: 80 OMR

16. TMJ/TMD Therapy

We diagnose and manage jaw joint disorders. Treatments include custom night guards, stress management, and therapies to relieve jaw pain and headaches.

Price: 100 OMR
