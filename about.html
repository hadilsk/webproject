About Us – SmileCare Dental

Welcome to SmileCare Dental, where our passion is creating healthy, confident smiles for you and your family. For over a decade, we have been the trusted dental practice for the Omani community, founded on the belief that everyone deserves compassionate, high‑quality care in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

We understand that a visit to the dentist can be daunting for some. That’s why our entire team is dedicated to making your experience as relaxed and positive as possible. From the moment you walk in, you’ll feel the difference that genuine care and modern dentistry can make.

Meet Our Caring Team

Dr. Evelyn Reed, DDS – Lead Dentist

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Reed is passionate about patient education and cosmetic dentistry. She earned her Doctor of Dental Surgery degree from the University of Pacific Coast and is committed to staying at the forefront of dental advancements.

Dr. Marcus Chen, DDS – Associate Dentist

Dr. Chen specializes in family and restorative dentistry and is beloved by his patients of all ages for his patience and meticulous work. He is a certified provider of Invisalign® and advanced implant procedures.

Sarah Jones – Office Manager

Sarah is the friendly voice you hear on the phone and the warm smile that greets you at the front desk. She ensures the smooth operation of our practice for over 5 years and is an expert at handling scheduling, insurance, and making your visit seamless.

Your journey to a better smile starts here. We are not just a dental practice; we are your partners in health.
