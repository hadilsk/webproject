About Us - SmileCare Dental

Your Trusted Partner for a Healthy, Beautiful Smile

Welcome to Smile Care Dental, where our passion is creating healthy, confident smiles for you and your family. For over a decade, we have been the trusted dental practice for the Omani community, founded on the belief that everyone deserves compassionate, high-quality care in a comfortable and welcoming environment.

We understand that a visit to the dentist can be daunting for some. That's why our entire team is dedicated to making your experience as relaxed and positive as possible. From the moment you walk in, you'll feel the difference that genuine care and modern dentistry can make.

Meet Our Caring Team

Dr. Evelyn Reed, DDS - Lead Dentist

With over 15 years of experience, Dr. Reed is passionate about patient education and cosmetic dentistry. Her gentle approach ensures that even the most anxious patients feel at ease.

Dr. Marcus Chen, DDS - Associate Dentist

Dr. Chen specializes in family and restorative dentistry and is a certified provider of Invisalign and advanced implant procedures. He builds long-term relationships with patients to maintain oral health for a lifetime.

Sarah Jones - Office Manager

Sarah is the friendly voice you hear on the phone and the warm smile that greets you at the front desk. She ensures the smooth operation of our practice and handles scheduling and insurance seamlessly.
